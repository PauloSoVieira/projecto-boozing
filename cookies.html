At Boozing, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, improve our services, and provide personalized content. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences, making your experience smoother and more efficient. Cookies can be session-based (deleted after you close your browser) or persistent (stored until manually deleted or they expire).

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functionality of our website, such as navigating pages and accessing secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect anonymous data about how you use our website, helping us improve its performance and usability.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences, such as language or currency settings, to enhance your experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: These track your browsing habits to deliver personalized advertisements relevant to your interests.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Some cookies are set by third-party services we use, such as analytics and social media platforms.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us to:

  • Recognize your device and preferences during visits.
  • Provide secure and efficient access to our booking services.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve performance.
  • Deliver targeted marketing and advertisements.

Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block or delete cookies.
  • Enable notifications when cookies are being used.
  • Restrict cookies to specific websites.
Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
